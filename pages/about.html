Browns Construction Electrical Services

Browns Construction Electrical Services are a Kent based electrical firm covering all aspects of the electrical trade. Our team are all fully qualified with the latest 17th edition regulations. We use only the best quality materials and take pride in delivering the highest standard of work at competitive rates, in a prompt timescale. Fully insured and certified with the electrical safety register, all of our work is tested and a certificate of safety is issued for both our records and for your peace of mind.
